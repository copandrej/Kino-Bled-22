Zdraviliški park, Bled

CHEESE AND JAM

Kajmak in marmelada
Slovenia / 2002 / 92 min / drama, comedy
Language: Slovene
Subtitles: English
Director: Branko Đurić
Cast: Tanja Ribič, Branko Đurić, Dragan Bjelogrlić, Rene Bitoraja

Summary: A story about a couple from the bottom of the social ladder, about smuggling refugees across borders and other 'suspect' things- it is, first and foremost, an attempt to tell a story about the worst in people, wherever they may be coming from.

Tickets: free entry

The screening starts at 21:30 with a short film Lana & Toni (24’).

In case of rain, the screening will be held in Festival hall (Cesta svobode 11, Bled).
