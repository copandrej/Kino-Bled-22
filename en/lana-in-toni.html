LANA & TONI

Slovenia / 2022 / 24 min / short, comedy, thriller
Language: Slovene
Subtitles: English
Director: Perica Rai
Cast: Inja Zalta, Andrei Lenart, Jure Rajšp, Leya Blackbird

Summary: Lana and Toni are in a long-term relationship. Lana wants children and a bigger family, while Toni lives in his own world and shows no signs of wanting to take a step forward in their relationship. One day, Lana and her best friend Maja go to the castle where Toni's birthday party is supposed to take place, but to their surprise, no one is there.

Tickets: free entry

In case of rain, the screening will be held in Festival hall (Cesta svobode 11, Bled).
