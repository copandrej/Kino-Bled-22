EJTI ŠTIH

Slovenia / 2018 / 57 min / documentary
Language: Slovene
Subtitles: English
Director: Gregor Bajt

Summary: Gregor Bajt's video documentary about the painter Ejti Štih was created in 2018 at the premiere opening of the ROJO Y ORO exhibition in the Manzana gallery in the painter's Bolivian home of Santa Cruz. The film reveals the background of the artist Ejti Štih while depicting the current topic - the right to abortion.
