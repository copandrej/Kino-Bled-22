FUTURA

Italy / 2021 / 108 min / documentary
Language: Italian
Subtitles: English & Slovene
Directors: Pietro Marcello, Francesco Munzi, Alice Rohrwacher

Summary: A portrait of Italy observed through the eyes of teenagers who talk about the places they live in and imagine themselves, torn between the opportunities that surround them, the dream of what they want to become, the fear of failing, the trials they hope to overcome.
