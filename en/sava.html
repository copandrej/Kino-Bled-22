ONE FOR THE RIVER: SAVA STORY

Ena za reko: Zgodba Save
Slovenia / 2022 / 84 min / documentary
Language: Slovene
Subtitles: English
Idea: Rok Rozman
Director: Rožle Bregar

Summary: It carves canyons, brings fertile soil, creates rapids, nesting places and deep pools. People drink its water, it is the inspiration for songs and arguments and a source of energy. The Sava River is a fluid connection between four countries and countless ecosystems. Join four kayakers as they paddle across Slovenia, counting birds and learning about its wild past, dark history and uncertain future.

Tickets: free entry

In case of rain, the screening will be held in Festival hall (Cesta svobode 11, Bled).
